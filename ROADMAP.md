# Aware roadmap

What ships today and what we're working on. Edit this file; `make roadmap` bakes it into the app.

---

## The app

| Tab | What you get |
|-----|----------------|
| **Map** | Safety map with nearby services, optional crime heatmap, long-press routing, and area insights. |
| **Safety** | SOS, live location sharing with trusted contacts, and a check-in timer with local notifications. |
| **Contacts** | Trusted people to alert, with per-contact opt-in and address-book import. |
| **Profile** | Emergency medical card stored on-device. |
| **Settings** | Routes & map toggles, data sources, coverage map, changelog, roadmap, and privacy. |

**Live today:** Free personal-safety map with safer walking routes, crime heatmap where we have open data, SOS SMS, live sharing, check-in timer, and lock-screen Live Activities. No account, no analytics.

## Summary

Aware is a free, map-first safety app: stay aware before anything goes wrong. Every protective feature ships without a subscription. Crime coverage grows city by city; everywhere else the map still works for services, routes, and SOS.
